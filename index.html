About Us

Welcome to the High School Physics Club! We are a group of students who are passionate about physics and love to explore the mysteries of the universe. Our club is open to anyone who shares our passion for physics.

We meet every Wednesday at 3pm in room 205. Our meetings consist of discussions on various topics related to physics, sharing research, and planning events.
